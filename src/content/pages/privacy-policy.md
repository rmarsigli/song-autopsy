# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 13, 2026**

## Introduction

Song Autopsy ("we," "our," "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### 1. Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our site, we may collect:

- **Request logs**: IP address, browser type, referring URL
- **Usage analytics**: Pages visited, time spent, navigation patterns
- **Device information**: Screen size, operating system, device type

### 2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect:

- Personal identification information (names, emails, phone numbers)
- User accounts or login credentials (we have no authentication system)
- Payment information (the Service is free)
- Location data beyond IP geolocation
- Cookies for tracking or advertising purposes

## How We Use Data

We use collected data solely for:

1. **Service Operation**: Delivering content and maintaining functionality
2. **Analytics**: Understanding usage patterns to improve the Service
3. **Security**: Detecting abuse, spam, or malicious activity
4. **Performance**: Optimizing load times and server resources

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your data.

We may share anonymized, aggregated data with:
- Hosting providers (for infrastructure)
- Analytics platforms (for usage insights)

We will only disclose individual data:
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or prevent fraud
- With your explicit consent

## Third-Party Services

Our site may use:

- **Hosting providers**: Your data may be processed by our hosting infrastructure
- **Analytics tools**: Anonymous usage data may be processed by analytics platforms
- **CDN services**: Content delivery networks may cache our pages

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

## Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

- **Session management**: Maintaining state during your visit
- **Analytics**: Understanding how the site is used (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some functionality may be affected.

## Data Retention

- Request logs: Retained for 30-90 days for security purposes
- Analytics data: Retained indefinitely in aggregated form
- No personal data is retained long-term

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

- **Access**: Request information about data we hold
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Objection**: Opt out of analytics or tracking

Since we don't collect personal accounts, most requests are unnecessary. However, you can contact us via appropriate channels.

## Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

## International Users

This site may be accessed globally. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of data to our hosting jurisdiction.

## Contact

For privacy concerns or data requests, please reach out through the appropriate channels on our platform.
